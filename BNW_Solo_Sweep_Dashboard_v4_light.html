Program Growth Since 2021

BNW launched Solo Sweeps in 2020 and saw adoption grow steadily as participants embraced the Clean Swell app. BNW-affiliated cleanups now represent roughly 35–48% of all recorded cleanup activity in Erie and Niagara counties.

📈 All-county cleanups grew from 269 in 2021 to 713 in 2025 — a 165% increase. BNW-tagged cleanups grew from 89 to 250 over the same period.
